Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the liver disease detection?

+

Our ensemble model achieves 97.9% accuracy by combining multiple machine learning algorithms including Random Forest, XGBoost, and Neural Networks. This ensures highly reliable risk assessments.

What information do I need to provide?

+

You'll need to provide basic health information including age, gender, BMI, alcohol consumption, smoking status, physical activity level, medical history (diabetes, hypertension), and liver function test results.

Is my data secure and private?

+

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal health data with third parties.

Can this replace a doctor's consultation?

+

No, this tool is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with healthcare professionals for diagnosis and treatment decisions.

How often should I check my liver health?

+

We recommend regular monitoring, especially if you have risk factors. For high-risk individuals, monthly checks are advised. For others, quarterly assessments are sufficient.
